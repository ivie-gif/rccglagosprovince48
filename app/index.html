Ministries

Explore our ministries, dedicated to
transforming lives and spreading God's love through service and faith.

Virtuous Women

Empowering women to grow in faith, love, and purpose through Christ-centered fellowship. Together, we build a legacy of grace, service, and spiritual strength.

Men of Valor

Uniting men to stand firm in faith and lead with courage and integrity. We are called to be examples of Christ in our families, church, and community.

Youthful & Single

A vibrant community for young adults to connect, grow, and thrive in Christ. Discover your purpose and embrace God's plan for your life as a youth.

Jesus Teens

Guiding teenagers to build a strong foundation in faith and live boldly for Christ. We create a safe space to learn, worship, and grow together in His love.
